About us

Auto-Mart

Auto-Mart's commitment to excellence is reflected in the quality of their work and the satisfaction of their clients. Whether you need a thorough interior and exterior cleaning, paint correction, or specialized services such as ceramic coating or paint protection film installation, Auto-Mart has the expertise and state-of-the-art equipment to meet your needs.

One of the key advantages of Auto-Mart is their widespread presence across Sri Lanka. With branches strategically located in Colombo, Galle, Kandy, and Kurunegala, they are easily accessible to customers from various parts of the country. This extensive network of branches ensures that car owners can conveniently avail their services without having to travel long distances.
